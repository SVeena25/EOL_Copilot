Butter Chicken

A popular Indian dish made with tender chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients:
  1. Chicken
  2. Butter
  3. Onion
  4. Garlic
  5. Ginger
  6. Tomatoes
  7. Heavy cream
  8. Spices (such as garam masala, turmeric, and chili powder)
  9. Salt
  10. Cilantro (for garnish)
Biryani

A flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), and vegetables.

Ingredients:
  1. Rice
  2. Meat (chicken, mutton, or fish)
  3. Cumin
  4. Coriander
  5. Turmeric
  6. Red chili powder
  7. Vegetables
Paneer Tikka

A vegetarian dish made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and grilled to perfection.

Ingredients:
  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Yogurt
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Spices (such as cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, and red chili powder)
  5. Lemon juice
  6. Vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, and tomatoes)
  7. Oil
  8. Salt
  9. Chaat masala (for garnish)
  10. Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
